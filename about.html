About

pencil credits: David Shrigley for Flying Tiger

Born, raised, and currently based in Milano, a certain restlessness mixed with curiosity and love for making and creating led me to experiment with various media and take on different creative jobs, ranging from product to graphic design, from illustration and tattooing to bartending and textile design.

I try to draw everything I can from my travels around the world which are always a strong source of inspiration. That includes languages, which have always been a passion. (at the moment — Italian of course, fluent English and Spanish, decent French, and desperately trying to make some sense out of Greek)

Seriously competitive, curious as a monkey, physically lazy and mentally restless, and constantly trying to get over both through creative expression.

Drawing, painting, etching, carving, engraving, printing, and whatever else the moment suggests.
